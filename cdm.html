PROGRAMS WE OFFERED

BS Computer Engineering

Computer Engineering is an undergraduate degree program that combines the principles of computer science and electrical engineering to prepare students for a career in designing, developing, and implementing computer systems and hardware components.

BS Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineering is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. It prepares students for a career in designing, developing, and maintaining electrical systems and devices.

BS Civil Engineering

A Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering is an undergraduate degree program that prepares students for a career in designing, constructing, and maintaining infrastructure projects such as buildings, bridges, roads, dams, airports, and water supply systems.

BS Industrial Engineering

Industrial Engineering is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study and application of engineering principles to optimize systems, processes, and operations in various industries. It prepares students for a career in improving efficiency, productivity, and quality in manufacturing, services, and supply chain management.

BS Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study and application of principles in mechanics, thermodynamics, materials science, and manufacturing. It prepares students for a career in designing, analyzing, and improving mechanical systems and devices.

BS Electronics Engineering

Electronics Engineering is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study and application of electronic devices, circuits, and systems. It prepares students for a career in designing, developing, and maintaining electronic systems and technologies.
