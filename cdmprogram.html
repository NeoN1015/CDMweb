COURSE WE OFFERED

Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering

The Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering program provides students with a comprehensive understanding of the principles and practices of civil engineering. Students will gain a solid foundation in areas such as structural analysis, geotechnical engineering, transportation engineering, environmental engineering, and construction management. The program emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical skills through laboratory work, design projects, and internships.

Career Opportunities:

    • Civil Engineer
    • Structural Engineer
    • Transportation Engineer
    • Environmental Engineer
    • Geotechnical Engineer
    • Construction Project Manager
    • Urban Planner

Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering program combines principles of electrical engineering and computer science to prepare students for a career at the intersection of hardware and software development. Students will learn about digital systems, computer architecture, embedded systems, programming languages, and network design. The program emphasizes hands-on experience through laboratory work, projects, and internships.

Career Opportunities:

    • Computer Engineer
    • Hardware Engineer
    • Software Engineer
    • Embedded Systems Engineer
    • Network Engineer
    • Systems Analyst
    • Robotics Engineer

Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering

The Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering program provides students with a comprehensive understanding of electronic devices, circuits, and systems. Students will learn about analog and digital electronics, microprocessors, communication systems, and integrated circuit design. The program emphasizes hands-on experience through laboratory work, projects, and internships.

Career Opportunities:

    • Electronics Engineer
    • Circuit Design Engineer
    • Semiconductor Engineer
    • Telecommunications Engineer
    • Instrumentation Engineer
    • Research and Development Engineer
    • Consumer Electronics Engineer

Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering

The Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering program focuses on the study of electrical systems, electronics, and electromagnetism. Students will gain a strong foundation in areas such as circuit analysis, power systems, control systems, communication systems, and signal processing. The program combines theoretical knowledge with practical experience through laboratory work, design projects, and internships.

Career Opportunities:

    • Electrical Engineer
    • Power Systems Engineer
    • Control Systems Engineer
    • Electronics Engineer
    • Telecommunications Engineer
    • Renewable Energy Engineer
    • Research and Development Engineer

Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering

The Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering program focuses on the optimization of complex systems and processes. Students will learn about operations research, supply chain management, quality control, production planning, and ergonomics. The program emphasizes the application of engineering principles to improve efficiency, productivity, and sustainability in various industries.

Career Opportunities:

    • Industrial Engineer
    • Operations Manager
    • Supply Chain Analyst
    • Quality Engineer
    • Process Improvement Engineer
    • Manufacturing Engineer
    • Logistics Engineer

Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering

The Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering program provides students with a broad understanding of mechanical systems and their applications. Students will learn about thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, materials science, mechanical design, and manufacturing processes. The program emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical skills through laboratory work, design projects, and internships.

Career Opportunities:

    • Mechanical Engineer
    • Design Engineer
    • Manufacturing Engineer
    • HVAC Engineer
    • Automotive Engineer
    • Aerospace Engineer
    • Energy Systems Engineer

Bachelor of Science in Architecture

The Bachelor of Science in Architecture program focuses on the design and construction of buildings and spaces. Students will learn about architectural history, building materials, structural systems, environmental design, and urban planning. The program emphasizes creativity, critical thinking, and technical skills through design studios, architectural drawing, and computer-aided design.

Career Opportunities:

    • Architect
    • Architectural Designer
    • Interior Designer
    • Urban Planner
    • Construction Project Manager
    • Sustainable Design Consultant
    • Historic Preservation Specialist
